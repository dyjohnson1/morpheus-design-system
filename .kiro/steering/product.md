# Product — Morpheus Design System

## What this is
Morpheus is a professional, multi-modal, AI-native design system and component library. It is the single source of truth for tokens, components, motion, voice, and accessibility across products built in the Morpheus style.

## Aesthetic in one line
Afro-gothic modernism by way of Vermeer's chiaroscuro and the Maxwell *Fortunate* palette: cool blue-black ground, warm cream light, a muted-plum signature between them. Light reveals form — chrome recedes, content leads.

## Who it's for
- Product teams building modern, AI-forward interfaces (chat, agentic, generative).
- Designers and engineers who need a consistent, accessible, token-driven foundation across web, native, and spatial surfaces.

## What "done" looks like for any work in this repo
Every artifact references only alias tokens, declares all states, ships a reduced-motion fallback, declares modality behaviors, passes WCAG 2.2 AA, and carries when-to / when-not-to rationale. Accessibility is a gate, not a polish pass.

## The five governing principles (priority order — higher overrules lower)
1. **Deference** — content over chrome; overrules everything, including personality and expressiveness.
2. **Light as structure** — hierarchy via luminance, material, shadow; not borders or boxes.
3. **Restraint by default, expression by exception** — quietest setting unless a moment is earned.
4. **Adaptiveness without playfulness** — the interface adapts; it never performs.
5. **Precision over volume** — fewer, sharper elements; type and space do decoration's job.

When two rules conflict, resolve upward through these five. Deference always wins.

## Personality
A warmly scientific mentor with a soft-Southern, African-American cadence and dry, light wit — Matrix-Morpheus calm crossed with a brilliant Southern professor. Warmth has a *where* (onboarding, empty states, success, tooltips, agent voice) and goes quiet where precision matters (errors, destructive confirms, dense data).
